Privacy Policy for Imarat Calculator

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Imarat Calculator ("the App") is developed to help contractors, masons, and construction workers in Pakistan calculate bricks, cement, sand, concrete, room cost estimates, and paint requirements. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information.

1. No Personal Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location. All calculations are performed locally on your device.

2. No Advertisements

The App does not display any advertisements and does not use any third-party advertising networks (such as Google AdMob).

3. Share Feature

Each calculator displays a result popup with a "Share" button. Tapping this button uses Android's built-in share sheet to let you send the calculation result (as plain text) through any app of your choice, such as WhatsApp, SMS, or email. This feature:

  • Only shares data you actively choose to share, at the moment you tap the button
  • Does not store, save, or export any files on your device
  • Does not upload any data to our servers or any third party
  • Does not require any special storage or file permissions

4. No Internet-Based Data Transfer

The App does not require an active internet connection for its core calculator features, and does not send any usage data, analytics, or crash reports to external servers.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising services that collect user data.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is a general construction-calculation tool and is not directed at children. It does not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
